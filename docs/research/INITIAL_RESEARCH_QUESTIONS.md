# Initial Research Questions

1. What do current OpenID and VC rails already solve for issuance and presentation?
2. What privacy gaps remain even when selective disclosure is supported?
3. Which status / revocation approaches are realistic for a privacy-maximal v1?

5. How does the EU age-verification / EUDI direction differ from a stricter anti-correlation profile?
6. What verifier conformance requirements would materially improve privacy in practice?
7. Which parts of the design are technical, and which are governance or market-acceptance problems?
